---
title: Groups & permissions
---

Groups let you share connections and queries with multiple users at once.

## Creating a group

Go to **Settings → Administration → Groups** and click **New group**. Give it a name.

## Adding members

Open a group and add users by name or email.

## Sharing with a group

When sharing a connection or saved query, select **Groups** and pick the relevant group. All current and future members of the group get access automatically.

## Admin visibility

Admins see all groups. Regular users only see groups they belong to.
